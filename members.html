Ghangasrath

Occupation: Research & Development

Styles: Gloving

Location: Hawai'i

Bio: Francis Stone (born 1996), known as Ghangasrath is a glover from the 808 Gloving Community He currently resides in Hawaii. He is most well-known as being a founder of modern abstracting. Francis started gloving in late 2014, when fellow founders of MAD approached him while tutting. Early 2016 he joined the 808 LB and attended some popping and gloving events. Early 2018 co-founded the team AbstractRevoltion which was initially set out to be a skill tag for a rare style of show coined Abstracting. Later that year joined the team Intricate and Creative Environment. He currently is an admin for the World Wide Glovers group and is still going to events, judging and competing in competitions.

StarStreamTuT

Occupation: Social Media Specialist

Styles: Digits, Tutting, Isolations

Location: Texas, USA

Bio: Mckinnley Riojas (born 1996), known as StarStream TuT is a glover from World Wide Glovers. He currently resides in Fort Worth, TX. He is most well-known for being a founder of World Wide Glovers. Mckinnley first began dancing in 2009 at the age of 13 practicing hip-hop foot work. After being inspired by liquid and animation style dancers of the era he then discovered Tutting after watching a JayFunk video. Soon after, he would then discover gloving in 2011 from YouTube Advertisements from Emazinglights. Due to being so young and having a sheltered upbringing he didn't meet another glover or get his first lightshow until 2017. During that time he developed some gloving tutorials and videos for YouTube from the movements he would develop. It wasn't until he saw a comment on his Instagram account about Glover's Lounge that he joined a community in mid 2017. After he joined the lounge he made a for fun team called The Akatsuki also known as DAWN based on fictional characters from the anime Naruto. Soon after he met the glover's Rieka and Flowsonn through Instagram and then the formation and creation of World Wide Glovers would begin.
